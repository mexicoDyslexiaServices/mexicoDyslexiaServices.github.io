Our Services

Currently, services are only provided in English and reflect pricing for certified speech-language pathologist and structure literacy dyslexia interventionist.

No referral is required to book an appointment with us, however we recommend families contact their employer, embassy or insurance company to establish eligibility for funding or coverage for special educational services.

Assessment

  • A comprehensive psychoeducational evaluation used to measure the strengths and weaknesses of your child to provide diagnostic results (i.e, specific learning disabilities, speech & language disorders and executive functioning concerns). This assessment includes multiple steps to accurately assess and diagnose using standardized tools.
  • Full Case History: Family, early life & academic history
  • Diagnostic Report: Findings and recommendations for therapy goals and school accommodations
  • Parent Meeting: Discussion of findings, recommendations, and next steps
    • Price: Determined on individual basis

Therapy

  • Intervention Sessions: 50-minute individualized interventions systematically targeting phonological awareness, phonics, spelling patterns, and morphology session with Orton-Gillingham certified instructor
  • Caregiver Discussions: 10-minute care-giver discussions, homework handover, note writing, correspondence, and other administrative tasks associated with the care of your child
  • Resources: Preparation of therapy resources and home programming for your child
  • Flexible Location: Option of clinic, home, virtual or school-based (additional travel fees may apply)
    • Price: $125 USD per session

Consultation

  • School Consultation: Collaboration meetings with the child's school team to help integrate successful therapeutic approaches into their classroom setting
    • Price: $125 USD per hour
  • Family Consultation: Up-skilling parents on how they can best support their child's literacy development in the home setting
    • Price: $125 per hour
  • School Trainings: Up-skilling school teams on how to identify struggling readers and utilize Orton-Gillingham based approaches within the classroom for the whole group
    • Price: Available on request
